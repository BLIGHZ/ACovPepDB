Welcome to ACovPepDB

ACovPepDB is a comprehensive peptides database of anti-coronavirus peptides, which contains 518 enrties with 214 unique ACovPs manually extracted from research articles and public databases.

Introduction


Coronavirus is a virus with a high infection rate and a great impact on the human body. Whether it is the SARS-CoV in 2003 or the MERS-CoV in 2012, it has brought a great crisis to our lives. The SARS-CoV-2 (2019-nCoV), in particular, has had an indelible impact on the lives and work of people around the world. Among the various methods of blocking and treating coronavirus infection, antimicrobial peptides (AMPs) therapy is one of the methods that many scholars are studying now. In this difficult time of fighting the coronavirus, our research workers have a great demand for information about Anti-coronavirus peptides (ACovPs). While there are many databases on AMPs, there are no databases that focus on ACovPs. In particular, most of the current antiviral peptide databases have not updated too much ACovPs data, which makes many relevant researchers unable to retrieve too much information about ACovPs in these antiviral peptide databases. So, an ACovPs database that can bring a lot of ACovPs data is urgently needed.

We expect ACovPepDB will become an information portal, where many researchers can participate and contribute sequences. In the future, we will keep curating ACovPs and update ACovPepDB to meet new requirements and demands.
